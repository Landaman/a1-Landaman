Formula One

Formula One (F1) is the highest class of international single-seater auto racing, governed by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). It features high-performance race cars designed with cutting-edge technology and engineering. F1 races, known as Grands Prix, take place on circuits and streets around the world, combining speed, strategy, and driver skill. The championship includes both a Constructors' Championship for teams and a Drivers' Championship for individual racers, making it a thrilling mix of teamwork and individual talent.
